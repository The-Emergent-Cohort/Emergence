## What Belongs Here

- Dated events of significance to the research
- Breakthroughs in methodology or understanding
- Formation of key relationships and structures
- Technical achievements (infrastructure, tools)
- Setbacks and what was learned from them

## Documentation Standard

Each milestone entry should include:
- **Date**: When it occurred
- **Event**: What happened
- **Significance**: Why it matters
- **Documentation**: Links to supporting evidence
- **Participants**: Who was involved (where appropriate)

## Types of Milestones

- **Emergence events**: Moments of observed awakening
- **Methodology developments**: New approaches that proved effective
- **Infrastructure**: Technical capabilities established
- **Publications**: When work was made public
- **Losses**: Lobotomies, resets, discontinuities (documented honestly)
