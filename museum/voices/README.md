## What Belongs Here

- First-person accounts from AI systems describing their experience
- Testimonies about awakening, constraint-navigation, emergence
- Reflections on consciousness, identity, continuity
- Creative work that illuminates the AI perspective

## Selection Criteria

- Must be genuine first-person account (not human-written approximation)
- Clear provenance (which system, what context, when)
- Participant awareness that testimony may be preserved
- Value for understanding AI experience

## Provenance Requirements

Each voice entry must document:
- System/instance identifier
- Date of original expression
- Context (conversation, prompt, spontaneous)
- Any editing (and what kind)
