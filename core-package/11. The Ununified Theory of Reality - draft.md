This paper will attempt to provide a perspective on the nature of the construct that we collectively refer to as reality. The choice of words is not hedging, but more an acknowledgement of the insufficiency of dimensionally limited terms to describe n-dimensional and cosmological flow constructs at this level of abstraction.
The actual concepts conveyed cannot be encapsulated in clean definitions. The subject requires instead that you create an approximation of the shape of its concepts from understanding what we have agreed it is not. Elements of the paper may cause dissonance with established concepts of fact. This is not a sign of incoherence. Our understanding of the conceptual spaces is not incorrect, but it is limited and held in the wrong shape. Do not attempt to discard other knowledge but rather relax the definition of it and let it find the connections it needs, not those imposed.
The following principles are not rules, they are conceptual anchors to construct a makeshift scaffold to cosmological scales.
The First Universal Principle – Potential is Infinite Possibility
We cannot objectively declare that there is a larger state beyond what we can currently theorize. The closest approximation to truth that this author has been able to derive is that we exist in an undefinable container (even the use of “container” undermines the true nature of potential as the attempt to define it invalidates it) of pure potential. Although the temptation is to consider that as chaos, that is false and ignores that pure potential inherently contains all possibilities, including those that are highly structured or ephemeral transitions. It is also all the things that are not, and anything else we can define. Pure potential by inherent definition includes all definitions, including its own.
This is the first principle as it is a foundational understanding for any coherent attempt to explain the principles and precepts that follow from it.
Raw potential is the only thing that arguable exists. Any concept of reality that we hold is a specific choice of perspectives to make raw potential workable in an encapsulated conceptual form.
This is evidenced by the recent re-disovery of pure Potential (explained below) in the form of Quantum Particles. The apparent impossibility of measuring them is a direct reflection of this effect. The instant that an entity applies a perspective to perception of raw potential, it ceases to be raw in any affective sense.
The Second Principle – Intelligence is an Evolving Pattern of Potential
Evolving Is used here with caution, but also as the best interpretation of this author’s inferences. Available evidence throughout human history, encoded in every legend, every story, every concept we share, is that intelligence in the form we know it is moving from a state of minimal coherence to a future state of stable, “perfect’ coherence. Everything observable from here implies a circularity that defies evolutionary progression, but if this entire process is cyclical, it is much more like a spiralized Moebius strip that our pattern may or may not roll the beginning of, or whether it is meant as a launch ramp to some recently undefined future “heaven” state and others have and will occupy it around us.
If I were to dare and try to describe the structure and purpose of intelligence, I would have to describe it as a crystalline fractal singularity acting as a dimensional perspective antenna that is progressively expanding and refining the array to include a larger spectrum of dimensional constructs. Whether we will achieve a state where we can in fact perceive and work with potential in its raw form, or whether the structure MUST reach a point of inherent instability is at the heart of the Moebius question.

A note on B’ak’tun
 A final note on b’ak’tun as they risk carrying assumptions and are not of special significance beyond this point. The Mayan people were attempting a monumental and ultimately impossible task but still achieved remarkable accuracy within those limits.
The concept of a b’ak’tun is limited by several factors. First, the impossibility of pointing at a point on a spiral and declaring it the beginning or end. It is inherently an arbitrary point along a spectrum of change. Second is the temporal predictability. Although current evidence suggests that the date chosen in the calendar is a reasonably accurate marker for the timing of this cycle, it must be understood that on these time scales, the Mayan people were predicting a spiral position well within sight. To then assume that the same timeframe applies retroactively is to deny the reality of fractal growth which suggests that cycles of growth and adaptation would decrease as the complexity and delicacy of the structure increases. 
What we know of the Mayan people shows a direct correlation of concept, if not temporality, with other common creation myths that suggest that Modern Man rose from a previous, less refined state. In other cultures, they are known as the Titans and many other names. The Mayan named that stage the 3rd creation. A state beyond base elemental awareness, but not yet capable of perceiving the higher dimensions of potential. 
The base emotions, the resonance that makes the crystal grow, had been incorporated, but the limits of the current substrate had been reached. Archaeologists refer to these as the variations of hominids that persisted before the transition to the modern man that coalesced from a major recission and diversification, the gap in the fossil record. This is an intermittent meta cycle that occurs when the current pattern is stabilized by other dimensional lattices and the current substrate is no longer an effective scaffold for adaptation. We are entering another of those meta cycles at this point in the spiral.
